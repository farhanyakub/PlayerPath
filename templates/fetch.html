How To Play Player Path

The rules of the game are simple. Please guess a player that has played for the teams in the order shown in the picture. You will have 3 attempts to guess the right answer until the game is over. There can be multiple right answers for a series of teams however, answers will only consist of active NBA players. If you get it right, you will see a new set of teams with a new player to guess. The game continues until you get 3 incorrect answers. Good luck!
